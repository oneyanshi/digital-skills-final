As a student majoring in broadcast journalism and minoring in computer science at American University, I am interested in journalistic writing, learning about the way we interact with (and react to) media and finding ways to make media more accessible. I believe that in today’s world it is imperative that people have access to a wide variety of news sources and that the media is adapting to the ever-changing technological environment.

I believe that journalism and computer science will lead to innovation: new ways to look at the world, new ways to interpret the world, and new ways to tell a story to the world.
